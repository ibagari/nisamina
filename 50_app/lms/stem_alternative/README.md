## What this envir is

Beyond the 6 culture-and-language envirs (garicomm + belize + honduras + guatemala + nicaragua + svg_yurumein), this envir provides a **STEM-alternative track** that lets Garifuna learners access first-world STEM curriculum in their heritage language. Closes the achievement-gap surface that no single MOE in the 5 Central American countries can resource alone.

**Per F-055 axis #1 sovereign presentation:** matter belongs to publishers; **presentation belongs to Nisamina**. The transformative work is Garifuna-language-overlay + Caribbean-ecological-anchoring + cultural-lens-framing — not republishing the source material.

## Trilingual (per F-066)

Per director directive 2026-05-23 (F-066 amendment): STEM track is **trilingual** (Garifuna + English + Spanish), not bilingual. The 3-language overlay enables:

1. **Garifuna** — heritage-language anchoring + identity-relevant content surfacing (per F-059 D-MAX-5)
2. **English** — source-publication language for most of the corpus
3. **Spanish** — primary instructional language for Belize/Honduras/Guatemala/Nicaragua public schools

Default-rendered language is per-envir-default (e.g., Spanish for HND cohorts, Garifuna+English for Belize). Per-learner language toggle on every surface.
